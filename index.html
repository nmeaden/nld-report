Classic Encounters
Iconic Matches
Spurs 1-0 Arsenal
12 December 1992 · White Hart Lane
1–0
Tottenham 1 — 0 Arsenal
The very first Premier League North London Derby. Paul Allen scored the only goal on 20 minutes at a packed White Hart Lane (33,707). A symbolic Spurs win to open the PL era — though Arsenal would ultimately come to dominate it.
Title at WHL
25 April 2004 · White Hart Lane
2–2
Tottenham 2 — 2 Arsenal
The Invincibles win the league at Spurs' ground for the second time. Arsenal led 2-0 (Vieira, Pires) before Redknapp and Keane (90+4' pen) drew it level. The point was enough — Arsenal celebrated a title they hadn't lost all season at the home of their greatest rivals.
9-Goal Thriller
13 November 2004 · White Hart Lane
4–5
Tottenham 4 — 5 Arsenal
The highest-scoring PL North London Derby ever. Arsenal came from behind three times. Henry, Lauren (pen), Vieira, Ljungberg and Pires for Arsenal; Naybet, Defoe, King and Kanouté for Spurs. Nine goals, four lead changes, pure chaos — the definitive NLD spectacle.
The 4-4
29 October 2008 · Emirates Stadium
4–4
Arsenal 4 — 4 Tottenham
Bentley's audacious 30-yard lob opened the scoring. Arsenal fought back to 4-2 (Silvestre, Gallas, Adebayor, Van Persie) before Bent, Jenas and a 94th-minute Lennon leveller. Considered one of the most dramatic Premier League derbies ever played.
Back-to-Back 5-2s
Feb 2012 & Nov 2012 · Emirates Stadium
5–2 × 2
Arsenal 5 — 2 Tottenham (Twice)
Consecutive home routs in the same calendar year. Feb 2012: Van Persie (2), Sagna, Rosický, Walcott (2). Nov 2012: Mertesacker, Podolski, Giroud, Cazorla, Walcott — with Adebayor and Bale scoring for the visitors. Still the two biggest Arsenal wins in PL NLD history.
