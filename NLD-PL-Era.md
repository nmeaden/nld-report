## 🔥 Iconic Matches

### 1. Spurs 4–5 Arsenal — 13 November 2004
**The 9-Goal Thriller** · White Hart Lane  
Arsenal scorers: Henry, Lauren (pen), Vieira, Ljungberg, Pires  
Spurs scorers: Naybet, Defoe, King, Kanouté  
The highest-scoring PL NLD ever. Four lead changes. Pure chaos.

### 2. Arsenal 4–4 Spurs — 29 October 2008
**The 4-4** · Emirates Stadium  
David Bentley's audacious 30-yard lob opened it; Lennon equalised in the 94th minute.

### 3. Arsenal 5–2 Spurs — 26 February 2012 AND 17 November 2012
**Back-to-Back 5-2s** · Emirates Stadium  
The only times Arsenal have won by a 3-goal margin in the PL NLD — twice in one calendar year.

### 4. Spurs 2–2 Arsenal — 25 April 2004 (Invincibles Title Clincher)
**Title at White Hart Lane** · White Hart Lane  
Arsenal drew 2–2, sealing the Premier League title unbeaten at their rivals' ground — for the second time.

### 5. Spurs 1–4 Arsenal — 22 February 2026
**The Most Recent** · Tottenham Hotspur Stadium  
Eze (2), Gyökeres (2). Arsenal's 5th consecutive PL NLD win, 8th unbeaten.
